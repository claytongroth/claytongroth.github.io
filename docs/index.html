North Dakota Wind Resource Facts

► As of 2017, North Dakota Leads the U.S. with an installed wind capacity of 2,996 MW
► As of 2017, North Dakota has 1611 wind turbines, being 11th in the U.S. for number of turbines.
► During 2017, wind energy provided 26.8% of all in-state electricity production in North Dakota.
► In 2017, North Dakota's wind energy saved an estimated 2.6 billion gallons of water.
► In 2017, North Dakota's wind energy saved an estimated 5.2 million metric tons of CO2.

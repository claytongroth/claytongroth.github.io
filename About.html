About Me

____________________________________________

Professionally

I am currently striving to grow in the world of cartography and GIS. My passion for using data to find the truth and representing that truth graphically continues to grow. Problem solving and critical thinking energize and motivate my work. The world of mapping brings all of my inclinations to the fore!

I have completed a Bachelor of Science degree at UW-Madison with a double major in Environmental Science and Philosophy. (Philosophy of science/air quality mapping). Beginning in the renewable energy industry, I put my skills to the test. This work experience intensified my interest in mapping and I knew that that work was what stimulated me most. So, I chose to pursue it.

Presently, I am employed as a GIS Technician and am concurrently completing a master's degree with the University of Wisconsin Madison in GIS Web Map Programming. I am extremely excited to be constantly learning and growing in this field!

Recreational

I have been steeped in the world of outdoor adventure since I was very young. I am thankful for that and it has led me to many fullfilling experiences. I am an avid rock climber (in case my content didn't tip you off to that already), but I also take the time to enjoy running, skiing, hiking with friends and family. I spend lots of time travelling to climb and I help out at some of the local climbing gyms.

Music is a strong passion of mine as well. I spend lots of time collecting and listening to my favorite music. I also dabble in creating a little music of my own!
